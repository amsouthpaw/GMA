Product

Guard My Angel is a geo-location personal safety application that helps you feel safer be safer everywhere you go.

Guard My Angel watches over you without compromising you privacy and automatically detects emergency situations. In the times of need it alerts you pre-selected contacts with the information they need to provide assistance.

Before you’re heading out, you set a timer for approximately how long the activity should take, and when you’re done you simply indicate that you are OK. If you don’t or if you pressed the SOS button, or if we detect accident forces, you pre-selected contact will immediately receive notification with a link to Google map which tracks the phone to the last traceable location.

Privacy

Is my location information available before I reach emergency state?

No, we provide access to this information only when we send the notifications to your pre-defined contacts. We also allow you to share your location information with your friends after you indicate you are OK.

For how long does the location information in the server is kept?

We clear trip information periodically for trips that took place more than 24 hours ago.

We don't delete trip information if you decided to share it with your friends.

Who can access my trip information?

After we send the notification, the trip information is available to anyone who has the link we published in the emergency notification you can forward the information to other friends by sending the link.

The link will not work after we delete the information from the servers – after 24 hours,
unless you decided to share your trip information with your friends.

Why do we ask for permissions?

We understand that we request you for some permissions. Here is a detailed explanation of why we need each permission. Also please take a look in our Privacy Policy where we detail our commitment to your privacy.

YOUR LOCATION - FINE (GPS) LOCATION

This permission is required so we can provide your contacts a map with your route in the case of an emergency. Of course we dont disclose this information to anyone unless you reach an emergency situation and we delete this information completely after 24 hours from our servers.

NETWORK COMMUNICATION - FULL INTERNET ACCESS

We require this permission so we can send the information we collect from your device to our servers. In case of an emergency we send a notification from our servers and not your device to provide a reliable service (so even if your phone breaks your contacts will still get a notification).

YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION - READ CONTACT DATA

We ask permission to read your contacts so you will be able to add contacts from your address book to your notification list and not add them manually.

PHONE CALLS - READ PHONE STATE AND IDENTITY

There are 2 reasons for this permission:

  1. We need to determine your locality (the language the phone is set to, and your country code) so we can send adjust automatically your local number for global use from our servers around the world.
  2. We save you the need to enter information about yourself (name/phone) in order to identify you to your contacts. (Note: not implemented at the moment)

SYSTEM TOOLS - PREVENT DEVICE FROM SLEEPING

This is a very important permission that allows us to collect your location even when the device is in sleep mode. This happens for example when you are not using your phone and screen is off. Without this permission we wouldn't be able to get your complete trip information.

Your contacts may get partial information about your location and will not be able to give you the help you need.

Location Information

How accurate is the location information provided during an emergency?

The accuracy of the location retrieved from the mobile device depends on many factors. The estimated accuracy information for your first and last location information in the map is provided in the notifications if you reach emergency state.

Can you capture my location indoors?

Yes, Location information may be retrieved from GPS satellites, Wi-Fi, and/or mobile network. When GPS based location is not able to be determined, we will try to get your location via other means (Wi-Fi and mobile network).

Why the map do not include my complete path information?

We do not capture your mobile location information continuously to save on battery consumption. We have worked hard to optimize the application to be as efficient on your battery as possible to enable you to run the application and use your phone normally otherwise for as long as you need it.

Notifications

Will my contacts get a notification if my phone breaks or shuts down?

Yes, the notifications are sent from our server and NOT sent from your mobile device. So even if your device is not working from any reason your contacts will still get a notification once the timer you set expires.

How many SMS are sent for each contact?

In an emergency situation we send up to two SMS for each contact in your list.

  • The first SMS sent as soon as the service identifies you are in an emergency situation, even if we do not have location information for you yet (which would usually only happen if you clicked the SOS button before you asked us to watch you).
  • The second SMS is sent if we received location information from the mobile device and the first SMS did not include location information.

What information is included in the SMS notification?

The first SMS includes the following:

On countdown expiration

  • If location information is available: < Your name > may be in emergency situation + link to map
  • If not: < Your name> may be in emergency situation + no location info yet.

The second SMS includes the following: New location received for + link to map

Does the application send SMS from my phone directly?

No, all notifications are sent from our servers. SMS are sent from a US number.

When does the 'Automatic Car Accident' detection trigger an alert?

Car accidents exert extreme acceleration forces on the phone. When you activate the application with 'Watch Over Me' and this option is enabled, we will monitor your acceleration and trigger emergency alert as soon as we identify abnormal forces that are typically observed during car accident. We will trigger an alert only when the forces exceed 5G-Force that are unlikely to occur when dropping the phone.

Settings

I am trying to add a phone number to the SMS contact list and getting 'Invalid number' error

You can enter phone numbers in almost any format. However, there are still some cases that the phone number is considered 'invalid'. When entering a phone number, the country code is fetched from your phone and then added to the phone number you entered (if the country code is not already part of the number). In case your phone doesn't have a sim card or for some other reason the country code is not available, you will get the 'invalid number' error.

To overcome this you can enter the phone number in the following format manually: + < country-code > < number >

For example, for the U.S number: 408-555-5555 enter +14085555555. If you don't know the country code, check it out here

Getting Started

'Guard My Angel' is a simple application that provides the comfort of knowing that someone will help you if you get into an emergency situation.

Basic Functions:

Panic Button
click the SOS button for an immediate help notification.
Watch Over Me
Click the Watch Over Me button when you'd like our service to watch over you for a duration of time you decide and configure.

When you are safe and no longer wish our service to watch over you, you can stop the service countdown by clicking the I'm OK! button and we will avoid sending any emergency notification to your configured contacts.

If the countdown expires without you stopping it, our service will send notifications to the contacts you configured in your set-up. Notifications will be sent even if the phone is dead or no longer in your possession.

Notes:

  • The service requires active Internet connection.
  • We will not disclose the information we captured from the mobile device unless you reach an emergency state.
  • The information that we keep during the time we watch you will be removed from our server after 24 hours and cannot be retrieved.

The Application has three main modes:

IDLE mode
The application is inactive and does nothing.
ACTIVE mode
After clicking the Watch Over Me button, our service will track your mobile device by collecting information from its sensors. The information is collected periodically for efficient battery usage and sent via the phone's Internet connection to our servers.
EMERGENCY mode
When you may be in an emergency situation and the contacts in your notification list will be notified.

Your notification list of contacts will received notice that you may be in an emergency situation and will be able to get information about your last known location and periodic locations from the point you requested our service to watch over you. Most importantly, your most recent location will continue to be updated periodically for 4 hours after your have reached an emergency situation or until you ask the service to stop watching over you.

Auto Emergency Triggers

Car accidents exert extreme acceleration forces on the phone.

When you activate the application with 'Watch Over Me' and this option is enabled, we will monitor your acceleration and trigger emergency alert as soon as we identify abnormal forces that are typically observed during car accident.

We will trigger and alert only when the forces exceed 5G-Force that are unlikely to occur when dropping the phone. Enabling this option does consume more battery, but it is optimized for minimal battery usage.

To Enable/Disable the abnormal acceleration detection you can simply click the Enable Auto detect accident check-box.

Passwords

You can protect your service with a 4 digit password for your safety. When enabled, it provides an extra level of security and requires the user to type in the same 4 digit pin-code to stop an active session.

When enabling the password, you will be asked to enter a 4 digit pin-code when you click on the enable box. You can disable it at any time, but if you are in an active session it will require you to re-enter your pin-code beforehand.

Reminder

When you ask our service to watch over you for any duration you pick in advance, you will get a reminder 5 minutes before the timer expires to avoid false alarms. On an android device the reminder will pop up in the status bar, vibrate your phone, and play an audible notification. The audible notification can be disabled for your safety if needed

To Enable/Disable the audio notification you can simply click the Enable audio reminder check-box.

SMS List

The SMS List page enables you to control the list of contacts that will be notified via SMS in case of reaching an emergency state.

Click on the Add button to either add your contacts manually or from your address book.

If you enter a name and phone number manually for a new contact:

  • Only valid number will be accepted
  • The number will be converted automatically to international format (E-164)
  • Note: The added contact will be added to your Email list, but will not be added to your phone contact list

If you enter a contact from your existing contacts on the phone:

  • Only valid numbers will be accepted
  • The number will be converted automatically to international format (E-164)
  • If the selected phone contact includes multiple valid numbers, all will be added to the list, and you can delete the one you do not wish to notify after added to the list

Click the red colored 'X' to remove a contact from your list

The number of contacts that will receive an SMS message during an emergency depends on the type of package you own. If you enter more contacts than your quota you will see a warning message and a button to upgrade. See more about upgrades here.
